Services¶

Use /services resource to retrieve services entitled in contract, status of planned and executed services and estimated time of arrival for trash pickup.

Methods¶

List of methods for this resource

Method Resource Description
GET /services List services entitled in contract
GET /services/plan List service execution details for services planned for today and next 3 days
GET /services/stats List status and details of executed services
GET /services/materials List hauling material information for services
GET /services/operations List operations information for services
GET /services/prices List price details for services
GET /services/{serviceID}/ETA Show Estimated Time of Arrival(ETA) of truck for your trash pickup
